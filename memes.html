Writing a Conclusion that doesn't suck (hopefully)

A conclusion should leave a strong impact on your reader, after all it is the last thing they see of your work. A conclusion shouldn't introduce any new information, in fact it should just be a summary of everything you introduced previously in your Essay.
The conclusion should accomplish 3 major tasks:

  • Restate your main idea or thesis
  • Summarize any subpoints of your essay
  • Leave the reader with an interesting final impression
